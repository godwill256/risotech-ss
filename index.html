What is Risograph Printing

Risograph printing is a high-speed printing of upto 130 sheets per minute that combines elements of screen printing and photocopying. It is commonly used for high-volume printing of receipt books, school reportcards, examinations, flyers, posters, newsletters, books, and other materials.

As the official dealer of Risograph printers in South Sudan, we are here to help you discover this unique duplicating technology. Whether you are a printing press, school or a design studio, a Risograph gives you the power to print bold, eco-friendly materials at large scale without breaking the bank.
